  2. Order Types
    1. We may, at our absolute discretion, accept and Order from you. Example of Orders offered currently:
      1. Market Orders – Market orders are instructions to deal now in your specified volume at spot price. When you execute a market order, your market order transaction becomes and Open Position which is subject to market fluctuations that occur between the time your market order is received/executed and the time your market order is filled. This means that your market order will be filled at the first available price that may be different from the quoted bid/offer price when you clicked to execute your market order on the electronic trading platform. By placing a Market order with us, you acknowledge that you are placing an instruction to open/close a Transaction regardless of the bid/offer price.
      2. Pending Orders – Pending orders are instructions to deal when your pre-defined conditions, for example, bid/offer price, are fulfilled. Pending orders are filled at the first available price which may be the same as the quoted bid/offer price when you clicked to execute your market order on the electronic trading platform, or a few pips away due to slippage. You acknowledge that your pending order is triggered only if your pre-defined conditions attached to your pending order are fulfilled. This means that your pending order will not be filled if your pre-defined conditions attached to your pending orders are not fulfilled and you can skip a Transaction entirely. You can change your pre-defined conditions any time before your pending orders are filled.
      3. Stop Orders – Stop orders are instructions to deal by opening or closing a Transaction when our quote reaches a level that is less favourable to you when compared against the current market price. By placing a stop order, you acknowledge that you are specifying a stop level and your stop order will be triggered if our bid/offer price moves against your favour to a point that it at or beyond your specified stop level. There are two types of stop order, stop closing order and stop entry order. A stop closing order is used to automatically close and Open Position when the market price reaches your specified stop level to limit your downside risk (a loss to you). A stop entry order is used to automatically open a position when the market price reaches your specified stop level to increase the probability of a profit to you.
      4. Limit Orders – Limit orders operate on a rationale that is the opposite of stop orders. Limit orders are instructions to deal by opening or closing a Transaction when our quote reaches a level that is more favourable to you when compared against the current market price. By placing a limit order, you acknowledge that you are specifying a limit and your limit order will be triggered if our bid/offer price moves in your favour to a point that it at or beyond your specified limit. The limit order level is the maximum offer price at which you are willing to place an order to buy or the minimum at which you are willing to place an order to sell.

融资融券交易中，杠杆指使用保证金增加/扩大交易数值。杠杆的表达方式是比率。举个例子，如果杠杆是50: 1，您将能够持有比保证金高出 50 倍的交易头寸。当您投资一种杠杆产品时，您的盈利或亏损将根据杠杆的头寸计算，也就是说，您为账户设置的杠杆比率决定了您的交易增加/扩大数值。

杠杆可以用于生成比从您的账户余额拨出进行开盘持仓交易（您的保证金）的金额高出多倍的回报。然而，进行可能带来更大投资回报的投资具更高风险。这意味着，如果价格走势对您不利，您将蒙受比从您的账户余额拨出进行开盘持仓交易（您的保证金）的金额高出多倍的亏损。

如果您不打算使用杠杆功能，请将杠杆比率设为 1:1。

保证金与杠杆之间的关系

杠杆比率指使用您的保证金（为进行交易从您的账户余额拨出并保留的金额） 作为"杠杆"进入交易的次数。杠杆比率的分母始终是 1。50: 1 的杠杆比率相当于需要 2%保证金 (1 除以 50 = 2%)。我们替您建立的杠杆比率将确定保证金的数额。杠杆比率与保证金成反比，您的账户所设杠杆比率越低，从您的账户余额拨出的保证金额就越高 （所需保证金）。

止损单的风险

系统按先进先出的次序，即按照收到订单的顺序处理订单，这是我们无法控制的。因此，您可能会发现很难或无法在某些市场条件下按照您的止损单规定的预期价位平仓。您设定的止损单虽然是有用的风险管理工具，却不一定能按照您原先设想的数额限制损失。在极不稳定的交易条件下，由于市场情况可能无法执行这些指令，您的退出价格具不确定性。简而言之，止损单是无法提供保证的，因为无法在所有市场情况下运作。

定期监测未平仓头寸

外汇和差价合约交易有很高的风险，Binary.com不会代表您监控您账户上的未平仓头寸。监控未平仓头寸和理解未平仓头寸所牵涉的风险，同时确保此牵涉风险与您的风险偏好相符是您的责任。由于隔夜未平仓头寸具相关费用，您应该考虑在您平常进行的工具投资市场中交易，而不是在差价合约市场中投资。

滑点风险

虽然我们将按照最佳执行职责行事，致力以要求价格对您的订单涉及交易进行开盘或收盘，但有时订单可能会由于波动率或交易量增加而受滑移。交易完成时订单价格和执行价格的区别被称为滑移。这意味着当您下订单时，您的订单没有依照确切的预期报价完成交易。滑移通常发生在股市波动率非常高或低的期间以及重大新闻发布期间或流动性有限的时期。滑移可分为正滑移 （对您较有利） 或负滑移 （对您不利）。

外部力量

您与 Binary.com的外汇和差价合约交易与一种标的金融工具相连接，您将承受与金融工具有关的价格、汇率、利率或其他市场波动率的风险，特别是如果市场所属的管辖权与你无关。通过电子交易平台在网络上进行的外汇和差价合约交易承受潜在数据风险，可能导致订单执行的延误。
